8 # Exodus provides further improvements to Babel. Instead of making users tell
 
9 # us what compute, storage, and queue services they want to use (required for
 
10 # babel calls), Exodus will automatically handle this for us. Callers need
 
11 # to specify what clouds their job can run over, and Exodus will automatically
 
12 # select the best cloud for their job and run it there.
